Yoga to me

To me, Yoga is a time spent to understand myself. We live a lifestyle in which everything is done in a hurry and with a high level of stress. This exhausting routine affects our mental and physical health, and often our relationship with the world. I learned to focus on each problem at a time and solved them according to my limits. I learned that I am strong and resilient.

I learned that teaching is my vocation. I learned that I must express myself to the world, even though I am a shy person. I continue to learn every day, as the practice of Yoga is based on daily experiences and challenges.
